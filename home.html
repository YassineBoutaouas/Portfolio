About Me

Studies

My name is Yassine Boutaouas. I have studied Animation&Game at the Hochschule Darmstadt (Bachelor of Arts). The study program has various disciplines centered around game development. While the program teaches a little bit of everything, it allows and encourages to specialize in a few disciplines according to choice - programming in my case.

Skills

I am a programmer who has mostly worked in the Unity Engine using C# as a programming language. Throughout my studies I have worked on several different game projects of varying scopes. I have developed gameplay systems, editor tools to author and debug different processes, different UI systems and AIs. Towards the end of my studies I have especially worked on the development of AI in games.

Internships

During my studies I have done a six month long internship at Studio Seufz. I have worked on the game Lucky Tower Ultimate as a graphics programmer and VFX artists.

Outlook

Regarding the Unity Engine, I am currently exploring the data oriented technology stack and the job system. In addition to that, I am learning to use the Unreal Engine in order to challenge my habits and to get to know another game engine. Aside from that I am practicing C++ in order to learn more about memory management, general code efficiency and performance.
